Here is a list of public, free-tier, trial, and developer credit VPS offers.

#### **Major Cloud Providers (Free Tier & Credits)**

*   **Name:** AWS (Amazon Web Services)
*   **URL:** [https://aws.amazon.com/free/](https://aws.amazon.com/free/)
*   **What is Free:** 12-month free tier for new accounts. Includes 750 hours/month of an EC2 t2.micro or t3.micro instance, S3 storage, RDS, and more.
*   **Limits:** 1 vCPU, 1 GB RAM. Usage is capped at 750 hours per month for the first year.
*   **Reliability Score:** 5/5

*   **Name:** Google Cloud Platform (GCP)
*   **URL:** [https://cloud.google.com/free](https://cloud.google.com/free)
*   **What is Free:** 00 in free credits for new customers to use over 90 days. Also includes an Always Free tier.
*   **Limits:** The Always Free tier includes one e2-micro instance (2 vCPU, 1 GB RAM) per month, 30 GB HDD storage, and 1 GB network egress. Credits can be used on more powerful machines.
*   **Reliability Score:** 5/5

*   **Name:** Microsoft Azure
*   **URL:** [https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free/](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free/)
*   **What is Free:** 00 credit for the first 30 days. 12 months of popular free services, plus over 50 always free services.
*   **Limits:** Includes 750 hours/month of a B1S Linux VM for the first 12 months. Always free services have various limits.
*   **Reliability Score:** 5/5

*   **Name:** Oracle Cloud
*   **URL:** [https://www.oracle.com/cloud/free/](https://www.oracle.com/cloud/free/)
*   **What is Free:** An Always Free tier that is quite generous and does not expire. New accounts also get a 00 credit for 30 days.
*   **Limits:** Always Free includes up to 4 Ampere A1 ARM cores and 24 GB of RAM (configurable as 1 VM with 4 cores/24GB RAM or multiple smaller VMs), 2 Block Volumes (200 GB total), and 10 TB of outbound data transfer per month.
*   **Reliability Score:** 4/5
